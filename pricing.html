FAQ
How do I subscribe after downloading?
Open AIVerify, sign in, and run a scan. When you reach the scan limit, you'll be prompted to subscribe. You can also go to Profile → Subscribe at any time.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Go to Profile → Cancel Subscription in the app. You'll keep access until the end of the billing period.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major credit and debit cards are accepted. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Does AIVerify work offline?
An internet connection is required to run scans. The analysis is processed on our servers to ensure accuracy.
What operating systems are supported?
AIVerify currently supports Windows 10 and Windows 11. Mac support is planned for a future release.
